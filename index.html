✨ How to Use Slay Ranker

  • Import CSV — Click "Import CSV" and select your leads file.
  • Map Columns — Match your CSV columns to scoring fields. Set to "(skip)" if unavailable.
  • Score & Rank — Leads are scored 0–100 XP across 5 dimensions and ranked automatically.
  • Explore — Click any lead to see their full score breakdown. Search to filter.
  • Export — Click "Export Ranked" to download a CSV with Rank, Score, Tier, and AI Summary.

💅 Tier System

💅 Slay (75–100 XP) 💖 Glam (50–74 XP) 🌸 Cute (25–49 XP) 🎀 Basic (0–24 XP)

📊 Scoring Dimensions

  • Care Readiness (max 35 XP) — Journey Stage + self-reported status
  • Lead Quality (max 20 XP) — Lead Status value
  • Source Quality (max 20 XP) — Lead Source + Channel Attribution
  • Engagement (max 15 XP) — Total Notes (touches)
  • Recency (max 10 XP) — Last Activity Time
💅 Map Your CSV Columns

Match each scoring field to a column in your CSV. Set to "(skip)" if a field doesn't exist.
